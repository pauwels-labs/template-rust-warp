Cloud

Cloud-ready software can be deployed anywhere at any time.

Many organizations and projects have existing deployment preferences, typically due to existing use or legal obligations on where certain things have to be. I will always attempt to accomodate these preferences and build software which can primarily be hosted in your preferred location, and as a secondary goal, could potentially be removed and placed somewhere else in the long-term.

I can also develop safe migration strategies for moving from one deployment strategy to another.
